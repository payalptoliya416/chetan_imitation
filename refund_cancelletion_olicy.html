Refund & Cancellation Policy

Learn about our cancellation, return, exchange, and refund policies to ensure a smooth shopping experience.

At Chetan Imitation, we strive to provide high-quality imitation jewellery and a smooth shopping experience. Please read our Refund & Returns Policy carefully before placing an order.

Returns Policy

Due to the nature of imitation jewellery and hygiene considerations:

We do not accept returns once the product has been delivered.

All products are carefully checked, packed, and dispatched. Customers are requested to review product images, descriptions, and specifications thoroughly before making a purchase.

Refund Policy

Refunds are not applicable for:

  • Change of mind
  • Incorrect product selection by the customer
  • Minor color or design variations (as products are photographed under studio lighting)
  • Damage caused after delivery
  • Delays caused by courier partners

Refunds Are Applicable ONLY In The Following Cases

A refund or replacement may be considered only if:

  • You received a wrong product.
  • You received a damaged or defective product.

Conditions

  • You received a wrong product, or
  • An unboxing video is mandatory (from sealed package to product).
  • The product must be unused and in original packaging.

Without an unboxing video, no refund or replacement will be processed.

Replacement Policy

  • Replacement will be initiated after verification.
  • If the same product is unavailable, store credit or refund may be offered.
  • Replacement shipping timeline: 7–10 business days.

Refund Processing

If approved:

  • Refunds will be processed to the original payment method.
  • Payments are refunded via Razorpay.
  • Refunds are completed within 5–7 business days after approval.

Shipping Charges

  • Shipping charges are non-refundable.
  • If a replacement is approved due to our error, shipping will be covered by us.

Order Cancellation

  • Orders cannot be cancelled once they are shipped.
  • Cancellation requests before dispatch may be considered on a case-by-case basis.
  • No cancellations are allowed for discounted or sale items.

Important Notes

  • We only support online prepaid payments via Razorpay.
  • Cash on Delivery (COD) is not available.
  • Colors and designs may vary slightly due to screen settings.
  • Chetan Imitation reserves the right to refuse refunds if policy conditions are not met.

Contact Us

For any refund or return related queries, please contact us through the Contact Us page on our website.
